Volleyball Club

During the start of my junior year, me and 3 of my friends planned to create a high school club called the Coppell Volleyball club. I was named president of the club because I had the most experience playing volleyball. The club would meet up every friday to practice for upcoming tournaments. We also hosted casual tournaments of our own, where people could come and have fun.
